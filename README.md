# Piece letter mapping

| Piece | White piece, white square | White piece, black square | Black piece, white square | Black piece, black square |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| King | `K` | `k` | `L` | `l` |
| Queen | `Q` | `q` | `W` | `w` |
| Bishop | `B` | `b` | `V` | `v` |
| Knight | `N` | `n` | `M` | `m` |
| Rook | `R` | `r` | `T` | `t` |
| Pawn | `P` | `p` | `O` | `o` |

Empty white square: `z`  
Empty black square: `x`
